## 🌐 About Me

I am a **Network Engineer** with 8+ years of experience designing, implementing, automating, and maintaining large-scale **ISP backbone, datacenter, and enterprise network infrastructures**. I specialise in multivendor environments, complex routing architectures, and high-availability systems.

- 🏗️ Led the **end-to-end migration of 14 Metro PoP sites** and **120+ B2B connections** between autonomous systems with zero major service disruptions
- 🤖 Actively bridging networking and software engineering through **NetDevOps** — automating with Python, Ansible, Jinja, Git & CI/CD pipelines
- 🔐 Proficient in network security disciplines including **firewalling, DDoS mitigation, RPKI**, and Nokia Deepfield
- 📡 Follower of **BGP & MPLS**. Speaker of **IS-IS, OSPF, eBGP, iBGP, MP-BGP, L2/L3VPN**
- 🌱 An eternal student — lover of **Juniper, open source, automation & ContainerLab**
- 📍 Based in **Lindau, Bavaria, Germany**
